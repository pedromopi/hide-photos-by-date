Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Hide Photos by Date respects your privacy and is designed to work entirely on your device.

Information We Access

The app requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to view, filter, and move photos and videos to Apple's Hidden album.

How Information Is Used

All processing is performed locally on your device.

  • Filter media by creation date.
  • Display the number of matching items.
  • Move selected items to Apple's Hidden album.

Data Collection

Hide Photos by Date does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal information.

No photos, videos, or photo library metadata are uploaded to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The app does not share your photos, videos, or personal information with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
